# AutoLeave - Leave Application Templates Reference

## Template Structure

Each leave application follows this standard format:

### Header Section
- To: [Organization Name] Administration/HR Department
- Subject: [Reason-specific subject line]
- Date: [Date placeholder]

### Body Section
- Salutation: Dear Sir/Madam, or To Whom It May Concern,
- Opening: Formal request statement
- Explanation: Brief reason (without excessive detail)
- Request: Specific number of days requested
- Assurance: Commitment to responsibilities
- Closing: Thank you statement

### Footer Section
- Signature: Sincerely,
- Name: [Your Name placeholder]
- Position: [Position/Department placeholder]
- Contact: [Contact Information placeholder]

## Leave Type-Specific Templates

### 1. Medical Leave Template
**Trigger Keywords:** sick, illness, doctor, medical, health, fever, cold, surgery, treatment, appointment, therapy, hospital, clinic, examination, consultation

**Subject Lines:**
- Leave Application - Medical Appointment
- Request for Medical Leave
- Health-Related Leave Request

**Tone:** Respectful, apologetic, brief explanation

**Template:**
To: [Organization Name] Administration
Subject: Leave Application - Medical Appointment

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to formally request a leave of absence for medical reasons. I need to attend to a scheduled medical appointment/procedure that requires [number of days] day(s) off from [start date] to [end date].

Accordingly, I am requesting [number] days of medical leave from [Organization Name]. I will ensure that all urgent matters are addressed before my leave begins, and I will coordinate with my colleagues to manage any immediate responsibilities.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]

### 2. Personal Leave Template
**Trigger Keywords:** family, emergency, personal, urgent, matter, private, home, relative, parent, child, spouse, wedding, event, ceremony

**Subject Lines:**
- Personal Leave Request
- Urgent Personal Matter Leave
- Emergency Leave Application

**Tone:** Professional, concise, minimal detail

**Template:**
To: [Organization Name] Administration
Subject: Personal Leave Request

Dear Sir/Madam,

I would like to formally request a leave of absence due to urgent personal matters that require my immediate attention. I need to be away from [start date] to [end date] for [number of days] day(s).

Therefore, I am applying for [number] days of personal leave from [Organization Name]. I will complete all pending tasks before my departure and ensure a smooth transition of responsibilities to my colleagues.

I appreciate your understanding.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]

### 3. Vacation/Annual Leave Template
**Trigger Keywords:** vacation, holiday, break, travel, trip, rest, relaxation, annual leave, time off, getaway, leisure, recreation

**Subject Lines:**
- Annual Leave Request
- Vacation Leave Application
- Request for Annual Time Off

**Tone:** Formal, polite, appreciative

**Template:**
To: [Organization Name] Administration
Subject: Annual Leave Request

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to formally request my annual leave/vacation time. I would like to take [number of days] day(s) off from [start date] to [end date] for rest and relaxation.

Accordingly, I am requesting [number] days of vacation leave from [Organization Name]. I have planned my schedule to ensure that all projects are up to date and that my duties are covered during my absence. I will be available via email for any urgent matters if necessary.

Your approval would be greatly appreciated.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]

### 4. Bereavement Leave Template
**Trigger Keywords:** death, funeral, mourning, loss, passed away, grief, deceased, condolences, memorial, burial

**Subject Lines:**
- Bereavement Leave Request
- Family Loss Leave Application
- Compassionate Leave Request

**Tone:** Somber, respectful, brief

**Template:**
To: [Organization Name] Administration
Subject: Bereavement Leave Request

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to inform you of a family bereavement and to request compassionate leave. Due to the passing of [family member], I need to take [number of days] day(s) off from [start date] to [end date] to attend to funeral arrangements and be with my family during this difficult time.

Therefore, I am applying for [number] days of bereavement leave from [Organization Name]. I will return to my duties as soon as possible and will coordinate with my team to address any urgent matters upon my return.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]

### 5. Maternity/Paternity Leave Template
**Trigger Keywords:** baby, child, birth, delivery, newborn, expecting, pregnant, childcare, parental, adoption

**Subject Lines:**
- Maternity Leave Request
- Paternity Leave Application
- Parental Leave Request

**Tone:** Professional, informative, timeline-focused

**Template:**
To: [Organization Name] Administration
Subject: Maternity/Paternity Leave Request

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to formally request maternity/paternity leave following the arrival of my child. I anticipate needing [number of days] day(s) off starting from [estimated date] for [actual duration when confirmed].

Accordingly, I am requesting [number] days of parental leave from [Organization Name]. I am working with my supervisor to ensure a smooth transition of my responsibilities and will provide updated information regarding my return date as soon as possible.

Thank you for your consideration and support.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]

### 6. Training/Professional Development Leave Template
**Trigger Keywords:** training, workshop, seminar, conference, certification, course, professional development, skill, education, learning

**Subject Lines:**
- Training Leave Request
- Professional Development Leave
- Conference Attendance Leave

**Tone:** Professional, justification-focused

**Template:**
To: [Organization Name] Administration
Subject: Training Leave Request

Dear Sir/Madam,

I would like to apply for leave to attend [training program/seminar/conference name], which will enhance my professional skills and benefit my role at [Organization Name]. I need to be absent for [number of days] day(s) from [start date] to [end date].

Accordingly, I am requesting [number] days of training leave. The knowledge gained from this opportunity will contribute to my performance and the organization's objectives. I will coordinate with my team to minimize any disruption during my absence.

I appreciate your support for my professional development.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]
