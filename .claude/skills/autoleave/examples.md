# AutoLeave Examples

## Example 1: Medical Leave
**Input:**
- Organization: XYZ Hospital
- Reason: Need to undergo minor surgery next week
- Days: 5

**Output:**
To: XYZ Hospital Administration
Subject: Leave Application - Medical Appointment

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to formally request a leave of absence for medical reasons. I need to attend to a scheduled medical appointment/procedure that requires 5 day(s) off from [DATE] to [DATE].

Accordingly, I am requesting 5 days of medical leave from XYZ Hospital. I will ensure that all urgent matters are addressed before my leave begins, and I will coordinate with my colleagues to manage any immediate responsibilities.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Department]
[Contact Information]
